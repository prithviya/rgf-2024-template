About RGF

An Evening For Creator & Art Lover Meet Together

Rathinam Grand Fest (R.G.F) is a techno-cultural extravaganza that brings together students from all over the country to showcase their skills and talents.
With a variety of events, ranging from technical challenges to cultural performances, R.G.F promises to be a thrilling experience for all. Join us and be a part of this grand celebration!
