About RGF

Join us for six exhilarating days filled with innovation, creativity, and celebration!

Rathinam Grand Fest (RGF) is an annual festival that brings together students, faculty, and participants from various colleges and institutions across the region to showcase their talents and compete in a range of technical, cultural, and management-based competitions
RGF typically features a diverse array of events including technical workshops, coding competitions, cultural performances, business plan competitions, and sports tournaments. It serves as a platform for fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration among the participants while providing opportunities for networking and skill development.
